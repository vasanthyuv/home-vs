CYBER SECURITY






cybersecurity is the systems and processes put into place to keep digital content, particularly sensitive personal information, secure and safe from cyber attacks and malicious threats. We’re living in an era of technology, and everyone needs to know a little about how to keep themselves digitally secure. Whether you want to become an expert in cybersecurity, or just want to ensure your personal information is safe, start by learning the basics. Here’s what you need to know: Security professionals need advanced knowledge about how to keep digital systems of all kinds safe, but learning about cybersecurity is not just for experts. Anyone who uses the internet, on any device, should know how to protect themselves and their information, in order to prevent hacks, data theft and viruses.
